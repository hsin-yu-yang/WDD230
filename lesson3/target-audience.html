Website Planning Document

Target Audience Description

WeatherWise targets a broad audience of internet users who are interested in staying informed about weather conditions in and around the areas near Rexburg, Idaho, including community members and visitors. This website aims to provide helpful information to anyone who wants to know the current and forecasted weather for these locations.

Personas

Emily Jones

  • Occupation:Student at Brigham Young University-Idaho and part-time Barista at a local coffee shop.
  • Demographic:Full-time student at BYU-Idaho studying Communications Works part-time as a barista at a local coffee shop Enjoys hiking, skiing, and playing board games with friends Lives in an off-campus apartment with two roommates.
  • Goals and Tasks:Wants to stay up-to-date with the local weather so she can plan her outdoor activities, such as hiking and skiing, with friends. She also wants to know the weather forecast for her daily commute to and from school and work. As a student, she has a limited budget and prefers websites that are free and easy to use.
  • Environment:Emily is a busy college student juggling between her studies, part-time job, and social life. She relies on her smartphone for most of her internet browsing and communication needs. She wants websites to be easily accessible and user-friendly.
  • Quote:"I love exploring the great outdoors with my friends. Knowing the weather forecast helps me plan my adventures."

Scenarios

  1. Emily and her roommates are planning a weekend ski trip. She checks the weather forecast on the website to see if the snow conditions are good and if they need to bring extra layers.
  2. Emily wants to go on a hike with her friends, but it's been raining for a few days. She checks the website to see if the weather is clear for hiking or if she should wait for a better day.
  3. On her way to school, Emily notices that it's snowing. She checks the website to see if there are any road closures or delays that could affect her commute.
  4. Emily is working at the coffee shop, and a customer asks her about the weather forecast. She uses the website to quickly check and give the customer an accurate answer.
  5. I would like to know what kind of weather Preston City will have during the winter season when we make our trip up there.
  6. As a budget-conscious student, Emily wants to save money on her heating bill. She checks the website to see if the temperature is expected to drop and if she needs to adjust the thermostat.

Amy Martinez

  • Occupation:Owner and Manager of a local coffee shop
  • Demographics:42 years old. Married with 2 children. Bachelor's degree in Business Management. Has lived in Rexburg for 10 years. Enjoys hiking and gardening
  • Goals and Tasks:Wants to ensure that her coffee shop is profitable and that customers have a positive experience. She is also interested in staying up-to-date on weather patterns to ensure that her shop is prepared for any weather-related changes in business.
  • Environment:Amy's coffee shop is located in downtown Rexburg, and she spends most of her time there managing operations. She has a computer at her shop and frequently checks the weather on her phone. She values simplicity and ease of use in website design and prefers websites that are visually appealing.
  • Quote:"I want my customers to have a great experience at my shop, rain or shine."

Scenarios

  1. Amy is planning a coffee tasting event for her shop and wants to make sure the weather will be clear for the outdoor seating area. She checks the weather forecast for the next few days to ensure there won't be any unexpected rain or wind.
  2. During the winter, Amy needs to ensure that the shop's entrance is shoveled and de-iced for customers. She checks the weather and plans for the right amount of ice melt and shoveling time based on the expected weather conditions.
  3. Amy is planning to expand her business by offering a new outdoor seating area. She needs to consider the potential effects of different weather conditions and looks up historical weather data for the area to ensure the new seating area will be profitable and well-utilized.
  4. During a particularly rainy day, Amy's shop experiences a decrease in foot traffic. She checks the weather forecast and decides to run a promotional deal to encourage customers to visit her shop despite the rainy weather. She updates her website and social media accounts with the promotion to ensure customers are aware.
