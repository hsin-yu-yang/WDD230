Target Audience

The primary target audience for the Taiwan Taoyuan Chamber of Commerce website includes business owners and community members in the Taoyuan region.

Personas

Businesses - Any business owner in Taoyuan would greatly benefit from engaging with the Chamber of Commerce. It serves as a central platform for fostering collaborations, exploring inter-business initiatives, and gaining insights into the local business landscape. New business owners can also utilize the website to familiarize themselves with the area.

Community Members - The Taiwan Taoyuan Chamber of Commerce website offers valuable information for all members of the Taoyuan community. Whether they are looking to discover local businesses or explore employment opportunities, the website serves as a comprehensive resource for interacting with and benefiting from the local business community.

Scenarios

Opening a shop - Imagine an individual recognizing the potential for a high-end commercial cookware store in northern Taoyuan. Their first step is to visit the Taiwan Taoyuan Chamber of Commerce website to ascertain whether such a store already exists. Upon discovering the opportunity gap, they leverage the website to connect with local restaurants and assess their interest in such a shop. Encouraged by the positive response, the aspiring shop owner proceeds to secure loans, hire contractors, and establish supply contracts necessary for launching the business.

Improving business climate - Several local business owners in Taoyuan have faced challenges resulting from escalating rent, property taxes, regulatory fees, and competition from online commerce. To address these issues, they turn to the Taiwan Taoyuan Chamber of Commerce website as a means of reaching out to like-minded businesses and collectively expressing their concerns to the local city and county governments.

Licensing - Emily, an aspiring entrepreneur in Taoyuan, plans to open a small bakery specializing in organic pastries. However, she is unsure about the specific permits and licenses required by the local authorities to operate a food establishment. Seeking clarity, Emily visits the Taiwan Taoyuan Chamber of Commerce website to find guidance from experienced individuals who can provide accurate information on the necessary licenses and regulatory compliance.ms.

Needs supplier - Michael, the owner of a boutique coffee shop in Taoyuan, is in search of a reliable supplier of specialty coffee beans. His current supplier is unable to meet the growing demand, and Michael wants to maintain the high quality of his coffee offerings. He turns to the Taiwan Taoyuan Chamber of Commerce website to explore a directory of recommended coffee bean distributors and establish connections with potential suppliers who can meet his business requirements.

Needs contractor - Sarah, a retail store owner in Taoyuan, plans to renovate her shop to create a more modern and inviting atmosphere for customers. However, she lacks contacts with professional contractors who specialize in commercial renovations. To find suitable contractors, Sarah leverages the Taiwan Taoyuan Chamber of Commerce website, where she can access a directory of reliable renovation service providers recommended by other local businesses. Through the website, she can connect with contractors who can help bring her renovation vision to life.
