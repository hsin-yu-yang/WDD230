Design Principles

Proximity

Apple

Apple's website is good example of proximity in action. The navigation menu is grouped together in a way that makes it easy to find what you're looking for, and related products and features are placed near each other on the page.

Visit Apple

Alignment

Airbnb

Airbnb's website uses alignment to create a clear hierarchy of information. The search bar, navigation menu, and main content are all aligned to the center of the page, creating a sense of balance and order.

Visit Airbnb

Rule of Thirds

NASA

NASA's website often features stunning photos of space that use the rule of thirds to create a sense of balance and visual interest.
